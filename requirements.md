# Scholarship Assistant – Requirements

## Problem Statement
Many students miss government scholarships due to lack of awareness and complex eligibility rules.

## Objective
To provide a simple system that checks scholarship eligibility using basic student details.

## Functional Requirements
- Select student category (SC/ST/OBC/General)
- Enter annual family income
- Specify exam result (passed or not)
- Enter number of attempts
- Show eligibility result

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Easy to use
- Fast response
- Works on desktop and mobile

## Future Scope
- Integration with official scholarship portals
- Personalized recommendations
